I hold a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence (KU Leuven) and a Bachelor's in Computational Linguistics (Novosibirsk State University). During my studies, I had the opportunity to intern at Google's Gemini team (current), Bloomberg AI, Amazon Alexa, LIIR at KU Leuven, and ETH Zurich.

I was born in Ukraine and grew up in the Far North of Siberia, Russia. My background motivated my commitment to building diverse and inclusive workspaces. As such, I am a mentor within the Inclusive AI initiative.
